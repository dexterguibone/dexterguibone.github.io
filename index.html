About Me

Glad to always be at your service. My name is Danilo Dexter L. Guibone Jr. — a versatile creative professional with 6+ years of freelance experience in AI video creation, video editing, graphic design, and photography.

I am currently working as a freelance AI Video Creator & Editor, producing cutting-edge AI-generated content and UGC-style videos for brands and content creators worldwide using tools like HeyGen, Runway, and ElevenLabs.

In video editing, I transform raw footage into compelling visual narratives — from viral TikTok clips to long-form YouTube content, podcasts, and promotional videos.

As a graphic designer, I have created logos, brand identities, YouTube thumbnails, and marketing collateral that communicate a brand's true essence and stop the scroll.

I have had the privilege of working with small businesses, startups, podcasters, and individuals across the globe — each engagement sharpening my creative and collaborative skills.

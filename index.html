Who I Am

About Me

Glad to always be at your service. My name is Danilo Dexter L. Guibone Jr., and I am a versatile creative professional with four years of freelance experience spanning graphic design, video editing, and photography.

As a graphic designer, I have worked on logo designs, brand identities, marketing collateral, and digital illustrations — creating visually impactful work that communicates a brand's true essence.

In video editing, I transform raw footage into compelling visual narratives — commercials, promotional videos, event highlights, and viral short-form content for TikTok and YouTube.

Photography is another passion of mine. From portraits and product shots to landscapes, I bring a creative eye and meticulous post-processing to every frame.

I have had the privilege of working with small businesses, startups, non-profits, and individuals across the globe — each engagement sharpening my communication, collaboration, and creative skills.
